Kazuya Mishima

Kazuya Mishima is the second to last character to be introduced into Smash Brothers Ultimate. Origionating from the Tekken series, Kazuya is regarded as one of the best fighters in Ultimate.

My Best Mains

My absolute best in SSBU are Kazuya, Cloud, and Incineroar. Those 3 characters have all the tools I enjoy using in a match therefore I have put in the time to get good at them. My top 6 are listed in order below:

  • Kazuya
  • Incineroar
  • Cloud
  • Corrin
  • Steve
  • Ryu

Kazuya's Electric Wind God Fist

Kazuya excels at taking out the other fighter by only landing one move to start off a combo. This move is called Electric Wind God Fist which is requires a tricky to time input but is fast, fully invincible, and sets up kill combos very well.

Cloud's Buster Sword

Clouds biggest strength is his massive sword which I use to keep opponents far away from me. He also has a mechanic called "Limit Break" which gives Cloud boosted speed and access to powerful moves.

Incineroars Brute Strength

Incineroar doesn't have any unique abilities, but his sheer damage output and heavy moves click with me to make him one of my best. Incineroar has fast moves and a versatile recovery, his only weakness is having the slowest run speed in the game.

More On My top 3

All of my top three fighers I believe could get me a top 3 placing in a local tournament in Jackson. Sadly, Jackson has no more Smash Ultimate tournaments upon E-Gamers Cafe shutting theirs down. Instead, I'm left bragging that I'm probably one of the best players in the city with Kazuya Cloud and Incineroar.
